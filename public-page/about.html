Quarantine Cuisine.

You heard it here first.

Mission

Dinner party was created within the framework of the Swiss Versus Virus Hackaton to overcome loneliness during quarantine due to social distancing from friends and family. 

Our mission is to reconnect friends, families and chefs by sharing quality time cooking meals together.

Our chef-led cooking classes bring you together with your friends or strangers into the kitchen of your favourite chefs and restaurants to learn new skills from your home.

You think you’re a Guest?

So, dinner parties? It’s been three weeks and we’re already forgetting how to cook, how to talk and how to interact. We’ve watched more cooking sessions and exercise videos from our sofa than actually participating in cooking and well… working out. We figured out that this quarantine thing is going to last for a while - we’re that smart - and, well, we miss our friends, our families, our nights spent in the kitchen and our limitless conversations around the table.

Right now (02:26AM , 05/04/2020), we’re being told by the world this time inside is a blessing. “Fix your bad habits!”, “Get fit!”, “Build the business you dreamed of!” “What have you been wasting your time NOT doing?!” - well us? We’re wasting our time sat at home, binge watching, binge eating… just all round enjoying the sloth life.

THIS IS AN INTERVENTION

Yes. An intervention. And well… we’re not sorry. We’re saviours. Yes you’re welcome and you don’t even know what this does yet.

But you’re still reading this.

Basically, you’re going to become a professional chef, you’re going to host dinner parties with your friends and you’re going to do that here *nods head* -

what we can’t help you with is the ebook recipe thing that you ask everyone to download so that you can work from your sofa for the rest of your life.

So, you’re a Chef?

We understand… actually we don’t. We can’t begin to imagine how hard it must be to have your restaurant forced to close.

We don’t want anything to stop you from bringing your food, your style and your spirit to the table - not now, not ever.

Here, we give you the space to teach your culinary skills to guests that want to learn with you how to cook food for the soul, to entertain and create dishes worth sharing stories over.

Our team is dedicated to fight isolation.
